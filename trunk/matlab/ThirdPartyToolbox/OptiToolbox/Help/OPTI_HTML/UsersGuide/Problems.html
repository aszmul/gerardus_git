Optimization Problems

OPTI Toolbox is designed to solve a range of common Optimization problems, ranging from linear through to quadratic and nonlinear problems. Additionally OPTI can handle both continuous and discrete problems, as well as a range of specialty problems.

For more information on the problems OPTI can solve, consult the following OPTI Wiki page:
